We've really gone and done it now. In a misguided attempt to pay tribute to two of the greatest Swedish music exports of all time - E-Type and Meshuggah, by mashing together one of the world's best songs with one of the world's best rhythmic patterns, we've managed to create something that can only be described as totally unreasonable.

But none of that matters when we're joined by Deadgirl, whose powerful voice makes it all fly!
