About the band

Raal is what it sounds like - screeching and howling, which gurgled its way out of Burträsket, Bygdeträsket, Västra Kvarken and Sävarån.

Raal was formed in 2023 and consists of Joel Lindberg, guitar; Nils Persson, bass; Kristoffer Pettersson, vocals; Mattias Syring, drums.
